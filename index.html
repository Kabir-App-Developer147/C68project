G. D. Goenka Public School - Awarded Best CBSE School in Lucknow, a CBSE affiliated institute established in 2011, is a part of a successful national chain of premier schools. We offer holistic education from Pre-Nursery to class XII in a safe, sound and healthy learning environment. Our campuses, Play School and Main Campus, are located in Mahanagar and Shaheed Path areas of Lucknow, respectively. We offer world class infrastructural facilities including central air conditioning and on-campus wifi, high-tech labs, well-stocked library, well-equipped activity rooms, digital classrooms, spacious playgrounds and swimming pool, cafeteria, hostel and much more to our students, in a high-quality co-educational setup.
